Smartphone Voice Source Localization based on TDoA

Supervised by Prof. Hongzi Zhu, in Institute of Network and Service Computing, SJTU

November 2015 ~ Present

In my Junior year, I join the Institute of Network and Service Computing and became a research asistant of Prof. Hongzi Zhu. The main goal of my topic is to develop a new method on smart phone to localize voice source. This can be used with bluetooth anti-drop devices to help users find their lost things quickly. When the bluetooth connection was lost, the speaker binding with lost things start to producing special signals so the speaker can be localized just by shaking the smart phone twice.

  • Design a new localization strategy using two microphones of a single smart phone based on TDoA

  • Invent a suitable signal pattern and corresponding algorithm to calculate TDoA

  • Extend the model from 2D to 3D

  • Implement the model on smart phone(Android OS) and do massive experiment in various scene

  • Excellent noise resistency, try to improve measuring distance and accuracy
